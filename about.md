Future Past Media started with a simple problem: priceless memories were trapped on formats that were fading every year.

I am a solo specialist focused on careful, practical media conversion for families and small personal archives across the greater Edmonton area. From intake to delivery, your project is handled by one person with one clear process.

## Why This Work Matters

Old tapes stretch. Slides fade. Cassette players disappear. The stories do not have to disappear with them.

When your media is digitized, your family can watch, listen, and share moments again without worrying about fragile originals.

## What You Can Expect

- Thoughtful handling and clear file organization.
- Honest timelines and direct communication.
- Output formats designed for real-world use, not technical confusion.

## My Process

1. Learn what you have and what matters most.
2. Confirm delivery format and project scope.
3. Convert and quality check each batch.
4. Deliver files and help you back them up.

If you are ready to preserve a box of old memories, I would be glad to help.
