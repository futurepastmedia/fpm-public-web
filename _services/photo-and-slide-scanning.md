Turn printed photographs, negatives, and slide collections into organized digital archives.

Scans are produced with clean naming and folder structure so you can find images years from now.

## What Is Included

- High-quality scans for photos and slides.
- Rotation and basic cleanup where needed.
- Folder grouping by event, year, or source box.

## Optional Add-Ons

- Metadata tagging for easier searching.
- Duplicate output set for family members.

## Best For

Families preserving albums, inherited photo boxes, and decades of slide trays.
