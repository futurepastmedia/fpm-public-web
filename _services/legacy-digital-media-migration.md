Move files from aging legacy digital formats into modern, accessible storage before hardware failure or media degradation causes data loss.

This service is designed for people with old computer media that is difficult to access today.

## Supported Formats

- ZIP disks.
- Floppy disks (3.5-inch and 5.25-inch where readable).
- Legacy optical media and select removable formats.

## What You Receive

- Recovered files copied to modern storage.
- Organized folder structure for easy browsing.
- A plain-language summary of what was recovered.

## Important Notes

Some media may be partially degraded. I will always communicate recoverability and options before final delivery.
